# Sample Natural Language Queries for Testing

## Basic Aggregations

1. What is the total sales amount?
2. How many orders were placed?
3. What is the average order value?
4. What is the maximum sales amount?
5. What is the minimum sales amount?

## Counting Queries

6. How many unique customers are there?
7. Count the number of transactions
8. How many products were sold?
9. How many unique product categories exist?

## Grouping and Analysis

10. What is the total sales by product category?
11. Show me sales by region
12. What is the average sales per customer?
13. Total sales by payment method
14. Count orders by region

## Filtering and Comparison

15. Which region has the highest sales?
16. What is the most popular product category?
17. Which payment method is used most?
18. Show top 5 customers by sales

## Specific Column Queries

19. What is the average unit price?
20. Total quantity sold
21. How many orders used credit card?
22. Average quantity per order

## Date-based Queries (if applicable)

23. How many orders in January?
24. Total sales by month
25. Orders per day

## Complex Queries

26. What is the average sales for Electronics category?
27. How many customers bought Furniture?
28. Total sales in the North region
29. Count of orders above $500
30. Average sales by product category

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific about what you want to know
- Mention column names if you know them
- Use phrases like "total", "average", "count", "by category"
- Simple queries work best
- You can ask follow-up questions based on results
